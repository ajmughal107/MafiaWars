MAFIA WARS

Offline Multiplayer Mafia Game

HOW TO PLAY

🔫 Mafia
Kills players secretly at night.

👑 Godfather
Leader of the Mafia team. Works with Mafia and helps eliminate everyone.

🩺 Doctor
Can save one player during the night.

🕵 Detective
Investigates players to find Mafia members.

🃏 Joker
Wins ONLY if players vote him out.

👥 Civilians
Work together to eliminate all Mafia members.

🗳 Voting
Players vote out one player every round.

🏆 Winning
• Mafia wins when Mafia members are equal to or greater than remaining players.
• Civilians win when all Mafia members are eliminated.
• Joker wins if voted out.
